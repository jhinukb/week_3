Moist Banana Bread Recipe

Baking Information:

This is the recipe for one of my favorite delicious moist banana breads. I love baking and I hope you enjoy this too! It is also a great way to keep busy during the quarantine!

Ready In: 1 hr 45 min

Serves: 12

Yield: 1 Loaf

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3⁄4 cups flour
  • 1 1⁄2 cups sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1⁄4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1⁄4 - 1⁄2 cup walnuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine the oil, eggs, sugar, milk, vanilla, and mashed bananas.
  2. Add the baking soda and flour.
  3. Combine well for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour into a greased loaf pan and bake in a preheated 325 degree F oven for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
